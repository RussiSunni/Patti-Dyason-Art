Information

Ordering paintings

To order a painting, please email me at patriciaanndyason@gmail.com, quoting the painting's catalogue number.

I will respond as soon as possible to arrange delivery and payment, and to discuss any other requirements you may have.

This website is not integrated with a payment system and cannot be used to order paintings or pay for them.

Shipping

Prices exclude shipping charges.

Deliveries to South Africa and Namibia are free of shipping. For deliveries outside South Africa and Namibia, I will contact you to agree shipping charges before despach.

Payment

Payment in full must be made in advance before delivery, except under special circumstances.

This website is not integrated with a payment system and cannot be used to order paintings or pay for them.

Availability

I make every effort to ensure that the information on this website is up to date. However I cannot guarantee the availability of any paintings or prints that you have selected.

Pricing, availability and other information on this website may change at any time without notice.

Contacting me

Please email me at patriciaanndyason@gmail.com if there is anything I can help you with.
